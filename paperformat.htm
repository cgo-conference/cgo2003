Paper format guidelines for CGO


Authors are asked to submit a manuscript with the following guidelines:
 

  1. No more than 5000 words, exclusive of figures and tables.
  2. No more than 23 pages in total
  3. 8.5x11" paper format, 1" margins, 12pt font double spaced
  4. PDF format


Along with your paper, authors will be asked to submit:

  1. A 300 word abstract-- please include this in your paper.  You will also be asked to enter this into the conference software web submission form.
  2. A list of keywords
  3. A list of potential conflicts consisting of: (a) collaborators (co-authors) in the past 48 months, (b) past graduate students, (c) M.S. and Ph.D. thesis advisors.


Authors may choose to either reveal their identities and affiliations on the paper itself or remain anonymous.  There is no penalty for either decision.  If you choose to reveal your identity, please include author names and affiliation on the first page of your paper.

If you have questions, please contact the program chair of CGO.
